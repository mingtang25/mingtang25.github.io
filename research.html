Federated Learning

    Federated learning (FL) is a distributed machine learning approach that enables multiple clients to train a neural network model cooperatively without sharing their raw data. In our reasearch, we mainly focus on addressing diverse challenging scenarios in FL, such as data stream, time-varying device availability, domain shift, resource-constrained devices, fairness, and data noise issues. We aim to propose FL frameworks and algorithms to effectively handle these scenarios.
